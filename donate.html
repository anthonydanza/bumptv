Here are just of the few things the volunteers at BUMP do to keep the lights on:

  • built and maintain the website
  • screen, schedule, and upload content
  • work with curators
  • film and edit shows, music videos, and beyond for community members
  • build custom props
  • provide access to equipment
  • broadcast live from events around the city (Long Winter, Bit Bazaar, Blackout Eid, Night Performances, to name a few)
  • run workshops
  • care for the studio space
  • plan events like this our telethon, our yearly Oscars The Bumpies, and more.


And all for free! It takes a lot of time and love - and the equipment, web hosting, and streaming are all recurring costs that we cover ourselves.

We want to nurture and serve our community for a long time to come and we could really use some funds to support that! <3 <3 <3
